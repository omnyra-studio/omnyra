## FIRST COMMENT — Maker Story

Hey ProductHunt 👋

I'm the founder of Omnyra AI, and I want to tell you exactly why I built this.

Six months ago I was running content for a small brand. On any given day I had 7 browser tabs open — ChatGPT for scripts, ElevenLabs for voice, CapCut for video, Canva for thumbnails, a separate caption tool, a research tab, and Notion to keep it all from falling apart. I was paying for four subscriptions, spending more time switching contexts than actually creating.

The content was fine. The process was exhausting. And every time I told a creator friend about my setup, they said "yeah, same."

So I built Omnyra.

It's a Creator OS — 12 tools on one canvas. You write a script, clone your voice in 30 seconds, generate a video, animate a presenter avatar, caption it, and ship it — without leaving the app. Every tool shares the same AI brain, the same brand memory, and the same seven thinking modes (Viral, Research, Truth, Creator, Strategist, Educational, Genius). Same prompt, radically different output.

What I'm most proud of: **Scripts and Research are always free, on every plan.** I was genuinely angry that basic writing tools were paywalled everywhere. So they're not here.

What I'd love your honest feedback on:

1. **The onboarding** — does it click immediately, or does the 12-tool scope feel overwhelming on first load?
2. **The Voice Clone** — 30 seconds of audio to clone your voice. Does that feel magical or does it feel like a risk?
3. **The pricing** — we're priced in AUD which is cheaper than USD equivalents. Is that confusing or refreshing?

We're a tiny team. This is a real launch, not a growth hack. If you find a bug, reply here and I'll fix it today. If something delights you, I want to know why so we can do more of it.

Thank you for being the community that gives a damn about what gets built. It means more than you know.

— The Omnyra Team

**Try it free → omnyra.studio** (no credit card, scripts always free)
